zREGEX

Depth Convert Your Surfaces without Velocity Model

About Plugin

The creation of Petrophysical Model i.e. Simulation of continuous data(e.g. Porosity, Permeability) throughout the Seismic Model, we need to have Seismic Data in Depth Domain in order to evaluate the amount of hydrocarbons in place in the reservoir.

Velocity Model plays an important role in Domain Conversion of Surfaces. It converts a Suface in Time Domain to Surface in Depth Domain which further used in Petrophysical Modeling.

zRegex is a Petrel Plugin that convert those Surfaces in Time Domain to Depth Domain without the need of Velocity Model or Velocity Data, so that users can focus on their Models.

zRegex takes Well Tops data to do the Regression Analysis of Time Data and Depth Data and by the use of that it converts the Surface input in Time Domain.
